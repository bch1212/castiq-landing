Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 24, 2026

CastIQ ("we," "our," or "us") operates the CastIQ mobile application and website. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our app and services.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address, display name, and authentication credentials. If you sign in with Google or Apple, we receive your name and email from those services.

Location Data: With your permission, we collect approximate (coarse) location data to show you nearby lakes and personalize fishing recommendations. Location data is never shared with third parties. You can use CastIQ without granting location permission.

Usage Data: We collect information about how you interact with the app, including lakes viewed, searches performed, features used, and app performance data. This helps us improve the service.

Payment Information: Subscription payments are processed by Stripe (web) and Apple/Google (in-app). We do not store your credit card number, bank account, or other financial account information on our servers. We receive only a transaction confirmation and subscription status from these payment processors.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the CastIQ service
  • Personalize your experience (nearby lakes, favorite species, stocking alerts)
  • Send you stocking alerts and notifications you've opted into
  • Process subscription payments
  • Send service-related communications (account verification, password resets)
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve app features and performance
  • Respond to customer support requests

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We share data only in these limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We use Stripe for payment processing, SendGrid for transactional email, and standard cloud hosting (Railway). These providers access only the data needed to perform their services.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, regulation, or legal process.
  • Aggregate Data: We may share anonymized, aggregated statistics (e.g., "10,000 anglers viewed Lake Mille Lacs this week") that cannot identify individual users.

4. Affiliate Links

CastIQ includes affiliate links to fishing gear and equipment through Amazon Associates and other retail partners. When you click these links, the retailer may collect information according to their own privacy policies. We receive only a commission on qualifying purchases — no personal data about your purchases is shared back with us.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we remove your personal information within 30 days, except where retention is required by law or for legitimate business purposes (e.g., fraud prevention). Anonymized usage data may be retained indefinitely.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encrypted data transmission (TLS), hashed passwords (bcrypt), and secure token-based authentication (JWT). However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Children's Privacy

CastIQ is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover we have collected such information, we will delete it promptly.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate personal data
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Opt out of non-essential communications

To exercise these rights, contact us at brett@vikinetic.com.

9. California Residents (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what personal information is collected, request deletion, and opt out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information.

10. Push Notifications

With your permission, we send push notifications for stocking alerts and fishing condition updates. You can disable push notifications at any time through your device settings.

11. Third-Party Services

Our app integrates with the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Google Sign-In: Google Privacy Policy
  • Apple Sign-In: Apple Privacy Policy
  • Stripe: Stripe Privacy Policy
  • Amazon Associates: Amazon Privacy Notice

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by posting the updated policy in the app and updating the "Last updated" date above. Your continued use of CastIQ after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
